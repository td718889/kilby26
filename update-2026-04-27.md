# KBP26 Guide Update — April 27

Less than three weeks out from Salt Lake City and the guide is coming together fast. [kilby26.netlify.app](https://kilby26.netlify.app) is live and actually useful now — you can search artists across all three days, filter by stage, and the whole rating system is wired up with autosave so nothing gets lost. There's even a Publish button so changes go straight to the site. It's a real thing.

The lineup's been fully rated: **102 artists across the weekend**, with 7 locked in at 5/5 and 20 more at 4/5. The S-tier is not messing around — **Blood Orange** (#16 all-time on Last.fm, Champagne Coast era forever), **Modest Mouse** (#39 all-time, and their new album *An Eraser and a Maze* drops June 5 — literally the week after the festival, so we're getting a preview), **Lorde** (Green Light through Solar Power, no bad eras), **Snail Mail** (new album *Ricochet* just dropped in March, she's going to be unreal live), and **The xx**, who haven't even gotten a take written yet but are obviously a lock.

On the 4/5 tier: **Flipturn** is quietly one of the most exciting ones — Serving Tray and Sad Disco are in heavy rotation. Also really looking forward to **Father John Misty**, **Japanese Breakfast**, **Lucy Dacus**, and **Magdalena Bay**.

Taylor's Takes are still rolling in — about 5 written so far, plenty more to go. Sunday's still getting worked through. More takes, more opinions, more strong feelings about set times coming this week.

Eighteen days. See you in the desert.
