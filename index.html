Official Kilby Block Party FAQ — pulled straight from kilbyblockparty.com/faq. Click any question to expand.
📍 Getting There
Where is the Utah State Fairpark?
155 N 1000 W, Salt Lake City, UT 84116.
How do I get to the event?
Your KBP ticket includes FREE UTA transit all weekend (May 15–17)! Ride all local buses, TRAX, FrontRunner, and more. Recommended: UTA TRAX Green Line to Fairpark Station. More info at rideuta.com.
How do I park at the event?
Very limited parking. Premium Lot (~250 N 1000 W, on-site) and Secondary Lot (1201 W North Temple, across the street) are available as add-ons through May 14. 3-Day passes include re-entry; single-day passes do not. Accessible parking: email accessibility@snspresents.com.
Is there a bike valet?
Yes! GreenBike Bike Valet at the east entrance — free (tips appreciated), open 11am to 30 min after last show. No bike? Use a GreenBike for free all weekend with promo code KBP26 in the BCycle app. Find stations at greenbikeutah.org.
Can I ride a Lime Scooter?
Yes! Lime Scooters are welcome. Park in designated scooter zones near the festival entrance — check the Lime app for closest marked zones.
🎟 Tickets & Entry
When do gates open?
Gates open daily at 11:30am. Each day runs until approximately 10:30pm.
Are single day tickets available?
Single day tickets and individual lineup times are not currently available. Check back for more info.
Is re-entry allowed?
3-Day pass holders only. You must have your wristband on to re-enter, and will go through security again.
Is this event all ages?
Yes! All ages welcome. Children under 2 get free entry. Tickets required for everyone 2 and older.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes, 24tix offers payment plan options. Select the Payment Plan option at checkout (available until March 2026).
Where is the wristband pickup?
More info coming soon! Download the official KBP app for updates.
🎒 What To Bring
What should I bring?
  • Your ID
  • Your ticket / wristband
  • Credit card or tap-to-pay (cashless event)
  • Empty plastic or aluminum water bottle
  • Travel-sized, non-aerosol sunscreen & hand sanitizer
  • Earplugs
What is the bag policy?
ALL bags are searched at entry. Allowed:
  • Clear bags (plastic/vinyl/PVC) no larger than 12" × 12" × 6"
  • Small clutch/fanny pack 6" × 9" or smaller (doesn't need to be clear)
  • Hydration packs (must be emptied of liquid)
  • One diaper bag for parents with children
What items are allowed?
  • Prescription medication in original container (daily amounts only)
  • Soft-sided baby strollers
  • Empty plastic/aluminum water bottles (no glass)
  • Travel-sized non-aerosol sunscreen & hand sanitizer
  • Rain ponchos, earplugs, small battery-powered fans
  • E-cigs only (no extra juice/cartridges)
  • Portable phone power banks
  • Point & shoot cameras or cameras with detachable lenses shorter than 2 inches
What items are prohibited?
  • Chairs, stools, blankets, tents, coolers
  • Animals (service animals allowed with screening)
  • Outside food or drink
  • Glass containers of any kind
  • Professional cameras (lenses longer than 2"), monopods, selfie sticks, GoPro mounts
  • Weapons of any kind
  • Umbrellas, fireworks, drugs, large signs/banners, laser pointers
  • Aerosol, glowsticks, markers, paint
  • Roller skates, hoverboards, skateboards, scooters (mobility devices ok)
Will there be food, drinks, and alcohol?
Yes! Multiple food trucks, beer & liquor tents, and free water stations. You may bring your own empty water bottle to refill. Kilby is cashless — credit cards and tap-to-pay only.
Are there storage and charging lockers?
Yes! Reserve your storage & charging locker in advance through the KBP ticket link.
